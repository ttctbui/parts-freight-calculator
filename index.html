Expected Spreadsheet Format

Your FedEx spreadsheet should have a sheet named Tables or Tables (2) with this layout:

RowCol ACol BCol CCol D (index)Col E–N (Zone 1–10)
1Service Name—Invoice Amount—Zone headers
2–13——$1 to $25 … $701+1–12Rate values ($)

Each service block repeats this pattern. The importer detects blocks by looking for the service name in column A.

FedEx Shipping Zone Map

Shipping zones are based on distance from Santa Fe Springs, CA. Zone 1 = California, Zone 10 = Hawaii.
